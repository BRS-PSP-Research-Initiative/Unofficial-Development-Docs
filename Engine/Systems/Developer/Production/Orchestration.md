* Role: Orchestration
* Real life counterpart: Middle Manager at a Corporation
* Purpose: Be the "Brain" of each individual game module
* System Filename:
    * Battle System: param2.bin
    * Field System: All .sc files
* Duties:
    * Receive and document orders from the Game's Executable and other Systems
    * Decide the best ways to delegate those orders through reading both Implementation Instructions received from Generic Production Stack and its own internal Instructions
    * Instruct relevant Systems (includes the Game's Executable and the Assembler)
    * Fulfill and deliver produced Asset to a known memory region that requesting System will grab from
