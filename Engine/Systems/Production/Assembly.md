* Role: Assembler
* Real life counterpart: Parts assembler
* Purpose: Be the "Brawn" of each individual game module
* System Filename:
    * Battle System: param2.bin
    * Field System: All .sc files
* Duties:
    * Receive parts from Asset Production chain
    * Assemble complete Asset
    * Notify both the Developer (when reading with a debugger) and Orchestrator when finished
    * Doesn't directly deliver final produced Asset
